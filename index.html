About Us

Ocean Breeze Commercial Cleaning began in 1988 as a residential and commercial cleaning service. As clientele increased, we narrowed our focus and concentrated solely on commercial cleaning. This allowed us to devote our attention fully to one are and maintain the highest level of quality and efficiency possible. Our staff is trained and professional, paying close attention to the small details that make all of the difference.

Services

Ocean Breeze Commercial Cleaning provides quality commercial cleaning services throughout the Florida panhandle. Our clients range from small businesess to office complexes-both private and government to banks, churches, clinics, and so on. No job is too small or too large (currently, our largest complex is 60,000 square feet!).

Some of our services include but are not limited to - Green Cleaning, Carpet Cleaning, Floor Stripping and Waxing, and Restroom and Janitorial Supplies (many eco-friendly products are available).
